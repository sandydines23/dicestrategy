Dice strategy FAQ

The questions that come up most often around stake dice strategy and bitcoin dice games.

Does the Martingale dice strategy actually work?

Martingale changes the shape of your risk, not the underlying house edge. It can produce short winning streaks but exposes the bankroll to large losses once a long losing streak hits bet or wallet limits.

What is the house edge on stake dice?

Most crypto dice games run a house edge close to 1%, though the exact figure depends on the platform and the payout curve it uses. Always confirm the stated edge before choosing bet sizes.

Is bitcoin dice provably fair?

Reputable bitcoin dice games use a provably fair system based on server and client seeds plus a nonce, letting players verify each roll's outcome independently after the fact.

Can a dice strategy guarantee profit?

No. Every bet progression operates within the same fixed house edge, so no strategy changes the long-run expected value. Strategies only change bankroll variance and session risk, not the mathematical edge.
